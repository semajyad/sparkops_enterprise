# SparkOps Business Model & Strategy

## The Core Identity
SparkOps is a "Voice-to-Cash" SaaS tailored specifically for New Zealand electricians (Sparkies). 
The core value proposition is speed and reliability: "Generate a legally compliant Certificate of Compliance (CoC) and Xero Invoice with both landing in the customers inbox before the van leaves the driveway, even with zero cell reception." We also offer an quick and easy administrative area for business owners to manage their electricians and jobs and assign new jobs to their staff

## Target Demographics
* **User:** 30 to 50-year-old NZ Electrician.
* **Pain Point:** Hates administrative work, loses paper notebooks, struggles with cell reception in basements or new builds, delays invoicing which kills cash flow, hates typing, technically illiterate, hates having to type, wants to speak naturally, wants instant results, doesn't want to do paperwork after hours
* **Pricing:** $100 NZD per month.
* **Success Metric:** 100 active users generating $10,000 NZD in monthly recurring revenue.
* **Admin user:** 30 to 60 year old business owners who manage multiple electricians or accounts lady 40+ years old. They will need to be able to manage all the jobs on the go and assign more and check everything is in order
* **Pain Point:** Needs to track multiple electricians' work and invoices and CoCs as well as scheduling jobs. Very time poor and needs to be able to manage everything on the go. Often ends up doing paperwork rather than actually working on their business

## The Exit Strategy (The $10M Goal)
We are not building a lifestyle business; we are building a Data Asset for acquisition.
* **The Buyer:** Major electrical wholesalers (J.A. Russell, Corys, Radcliffe) or broader trade platforms (Tradify, Xero).
* **The Asset:** "Share of Wallet" intelligence. By processing receipts and invoices, SparkOps builds a real-time database of exactly what materials Sparkies are buying, in what volume, and from which competitors. 
* **The Pitch to Buyers:** "Acquire SparkOps to offer your 10,000 customers a one-click automated CoC tool, locking them into your material ecosystem."

## Marketing Strategy
* **Channels:** LinkedIn, Facebook groups, electrical trade shows, word-of-mouth referrals from early adopters.
* **Message:** "Stop losing money on paperwork. Generate your CoC and Invoice in seconds, even without cell service."
* **Tactics:** Offer free trials to electricians, partner with electrical wholesalers for co-marketing, create case studies from early adopters.
* **Method:** Marketing must be automated as much as possible to reduce operational overhead.
* **Goal:** Achieve 100 active users generating $10,000 NZD in monthly recurring revenue.

## Goals
* **Goal 1:** 10 active users generating $1,000 NZD in monthly recurring revenue
* **Goal 2:** 50 active users generating $5,000 NZD in monthly recurring revenue
* **Goal 3:** 100 active users generating $10,000 NZD in monthly recurring revenue
* **Goal 4:** Achieve $10M valuation through acquisition
